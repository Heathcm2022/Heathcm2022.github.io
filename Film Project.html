My groups video advertising the housing at Millersville.

In this project, we were instructed to create an advertisement for some aspect of Millersville University. My group decided to choose the dorms for our video. We started out with chooses our topic, location, and format of our video. We chose to use my dorm and have the majority of the filming be a silent walkthrough. After that, we had our narration recorded in a quiet room, and we spliced and dissected the film into place. We finished by dubbing some music in the background and adding credits. This assignment was a lot of fun because it gave us the opportunity to be creative and have overall full control of our video (as long as it fit within our criteria).
